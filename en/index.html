Biography

Natalia Jiménez Figueroa, was born on 9th of July in 1983, in Concepción, Chile. Her family consists of her parents, Carlos and Dania and her younger brother Lucas.

She studied in Wessex School and Saint John's School, in Concepción, where she realized her first musical performances. Later in the year 2000 she emigrates to Santiago to start taking singing lessons, with Ricardo Álvarez and Ana María Meza.

In the years thereafter she makes an album, under the musical production of Juan Carlos Duque, called “No Renunciaré” (I won't give up), where several songwriters took part, like, Nano Prado, John Elliot , Álvaro Véliz, Soledad Guerrero, among others.

The musical style of Natalia is romantic ballad and pop music. She have also incursionated in anglo ballads.

She has also participated in musical festivals in La Serena, Providencia, Paine, etc.

Professionally, she has made presentations in corporate events, Monticello Casino, Militar Club Santiago, Santa Cruz Hotel, Ritz Carlton Hotel, Grand Hyatt Hotel, among others.

Currently, she´s promoting , through the entire country, her show called “Tribute to the Greatest Divas”.
